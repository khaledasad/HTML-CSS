Using a combination of technologies that are being developed at Georgia Gwinnett College, University of Georgia, Georgia Institute of Technology, RocketsRUs Corp is pioneering the first in a new generation of space craft, the Pegasus space plane. Pegasus takes off like an airplane, from a concentional runway.
