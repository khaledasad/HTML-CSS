Nicole Kidman

Education

  • Success followed her with Billy Bathgate (1991), To Die For (1995), Practical
    Magic (1998), and Eyes Wide Shut (1999)

  • She also has worked with a assortment of different actors and actresses like
    Tom Cruise, Naomi Watts, Will Farrell, Hugh Jackman, and Jude Law.

  • Nicole Kidman has received several major honors for her work, including her
    first Academy Award as Best Actress.

  • To prepare for her role in The Human Stain (2003) she visited women's shelters
    and talked to former victims of abuse for inspiration.

  • To make the character 'Marisa Coulter' in The Golden Compass (2007) as good
    as possible, she read the whole “His Dark Materials” trilogy, and wrote small
    notes, whenever she found something personal about the character.
